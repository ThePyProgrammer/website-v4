I joined this project as part of [SUTD's Research Mentorship Programme](https://www.sutd.edu.sg/innovation/davincisutd/mentorship-programmes/research-mentorship-programme/), where I applied to work with [Prof Immanuel Koh](https://www.sutd.edu.sg/profile/immanuel-koh) from SUTD's Architecture and Sustainable Design (ASD) Pillar. We were assigned to develop a system to semantically segment features of HDB buildings, based off real buildings in a classified set. We finetuned models to conduct the semantic segmentation, and tested our systems against various different sample sets.
