I was one of the three Overalls-in-Charge (OICs) at [BuildingBloCS 2023](https://old.buildingblocs.sg/2023-newnew/).

During my term in BuildingBloCS, I was responsible for the large-scale Development side of BuildingBloCS, where-in we developed [Starvation](https://github.com/buildingblocs/starvation), an open source gamified coding platform inspired by [CodeCombat](https://codecombat.com/), with the gameplay influenced by [BattleCats](https://battlecats.club/en/series/battlecats/). Developed with Vue, Flask, PostgreSQL and the Google Cloud Kubernetes Engine, this game was designed for beginner students to learn the workings of Python by solving coding challenges and data skills acquired from the workshops within the March Conference.

In addition to my work as the Development Lead on Starvation, I led efforts for the March and June Conference alongside by fellow OICs, including liasing with external sponsors like FOSSASIA, Better.sg, AI Singapore and Temus. We established a detailed curriculum to teach students the basics of programming, from basic Python programming up to data analytics, deep learning and advanced AI algorithms like Transformers and Reinforcement Learning. I personally taught a few workshops on [Python Programming](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_sBc1KkuezU) and Flask-AI Integration.

I also largely led the management of the [June Conference Hackathon](https://buildingblocs-2023.devpost.com/project-gallery) and the [Singathon](https://singathon.devpost.com/project-gallery). We also "incubated" the first ever [Blahaj CTF](https://blahaj.sg/) in December 2023, advising the team with their logistics and planning.
